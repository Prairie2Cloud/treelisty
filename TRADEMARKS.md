# TreeListy Trademark Policy

This document describes the trademark and branding guidelines for TreeListy.

## Trademarks

The following are trademarks of Prairie2Cloud LLC:

- **TreeListy** (word mark)
- **TreeListy logo** (tree icon with hierarchical nodes)
- **TreeBeard** (the AI assistant persona)
- **"Think in trees"** (tagline)

## Permitted Uses

### You MAY use the TreeListy name to:

1. **Describe compatibility** - "Compatible with TreeListy" or "Works with TreeListy"
2. **Reference the project** - "Built on TreeListy" or "Powered by TreeListy"
3. **Create integrations** - "TreeListy Connector for [Your Product]"
4. **Write about TreeListy** - Blog posts, tutorials, reviews

### Examples of acceptable naming:

- `treelisty-mcp-bridge` (official package)
- `awesome-treelisty` (community list)
- `treelisty-export-plugin` (third-party integration)
- "My TreeListy workflow template"

## Prohibited Uses

### You MAY NOT:

1. **Use TreeListy as your product name** - No "TreeListy Pro" or "TreeListy Enterprise" unless officially authorized
2. **Imply official endorsement** - No "Official TreeListy Partner" without written agreement
3. **Modify the logo** - Do not alter colors, proportions, or add elements
4. **Register confusing domains** - No `treelisty.io`, `gettreelisty.com`, etc.
5. **Use in a way that disparages TreeListy** - No misleading or defamatory use

### Examples of prohibited naming:

- `TreeListy 2.0` (implies official version)
- `TreeListy Cloud` (implies official service)
- `BetterTreeListy` (implies replacement)
- `TreeListy Inc.` (implies corporate affiliation)

## Forks and Derivatives

If you fork TreeListy under the MPL-2.0 license:

1. **You MUST remove TreeListy branding** - Choose a different name
2. **You MUST remove the TreeListy logo** - Use your own branding
3. **You MAY reference origin** - "Based on TreeListy" or "Forked from TreeListy" is acceptable
4. **You MUST comply with the license** - See `LICENSE.md`

### Acceptable fork naming:

- `MyProjectTree` (completely different name)
- `HierarchyBuilder` (descriptive, non-confusing)
- `OpenTree` (generic, distinct)

### Unacceptable fork naming:

- `TreeListy Lite`
- `TreeListy Open Source Edition`
- `TreeListy Community`

## Logo Usage

### Official Logo

The TreeListy logo consists of a stylized tree icon representing hierarchical nodes.

**Logo files:** Available upon request for authorized uses.

### Logo Guidelines

1. **Minimum size:** 24px height for digital, 0.5 inch for print
2. **Clear space:** Maintain padding equal to the height of the "T" in TreeListy
3. **Background:** Use on white or light backgrounds; dark mode variant available
4. **No modifications:** Do not rotate, skew, recolor, or add effects

## TreeBeard

**TreeBeard** is the name of TreeListy's AI assistant persona.

- You MAY reference TreeBeard in documentation about TreeListy
- You MAY NOT create competing AI assistants named TreeBeard
- You MAY NOT use TreeBeard branding in your own products

## Attribution Requirements

When using TreeListy's open-source code:

1. **Keep license headers intact** - Do not remove copyright notices
2. **Attribute in about/credits** - "Powered by TreeListy" or similar
3. **Link to official site** - https://treelisty.netlify.app

1. Request modifications to non-compliant uses

We prefer to resolve issues collaboratively. Please contact us if you're unsure about a use case.
